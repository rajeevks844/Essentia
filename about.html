Essentia Fragrances Flavors & Seasonings Pvt. Ltd.


Incepted in the year 2012, at Delhi (India), we, “Essentia Fragrances Flavors & Seasonings Pvt. Ltd.,” are an ISO 9001:2008 & 22000:2005 certified firm engaged in Manufacturing and Supplying a comprehensive assortment of Essential Oil, Fragrance Oil, Food Flavor, Seasoning, Natural Food Color, etc. Our offered range is processed under the guidance of our skilled team of professionals using the superlative grade compounds and latest processing techniques. In addition to this, our team of procuring agents procures the best quality compounds from the reliable vendors of the market in compliance with the international quality standards. Furthermore, procured compounds are stringently tested on various parameters to avoid any kind of discrepancy in the offered product range. These products are appreciated for their properties like purity, rich aroma & fragrance, longer shelf life and accurate composition. The offered assortment is available in customized options at most affordable price range to our clients.
We are well supported by sound and sophisticated infrastructure, which is equipped with highly developed machinery and tools. Our large infrastructure is segregated into various departments like manufacturing, quality testing, sales & marketing, etc. All these machinery installed in our unit are upgraded on a regular basis by our professionals that facilitate us in meeting the precise requirements of customers within the predetermined time schedule. In addition to this, we also make sure that our products are packed in quality packaging material in order to retain their freshness and rich aroma. Bolstered by a team of experienced and knowledgeable professionals, we are engaged in offering a wide range of products. All our team members work in close-coordination with the customers in order to understand their specifications and instructions. Moreover, owing to their commitment and dedication, we are able to achieve the predetermined objectives of the firm.
Being a quality oriented organization, we are engaged in providing utmost quality range of products. In addition to this, these products are checked right from the procurement of raw material till the final dispatch at the clients’ end.
