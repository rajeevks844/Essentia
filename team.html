Mr. Manish Kumar

Founder



Mr. Manish Kumar is the visionary founder of Essentia Fragrances, Flavors, and Seasonings Pvt. Ltd., a leading company in the manufacturing and supply of a wide array of products, including essential oils, fragrance oils, food flavors, seasonings, and natural food colors. Under his leadership, Essentia has established itself as a trusted name in the industry, known for its commitment to quality, innovation, and sustainability. Mr. Kumar's deep understanding of the market and customer needs has been instrumental in driving the company's success and its mission to deliver exceptional products that enhance the sensory experience across various industries.

Essentia Fragrances Flavors And Seasonings Pvt. Ltd.

Incepted in the year 2012, at Delhi (India), we, “Essentia Fragrances Flavors & Seasonings Pvt. Ltd.,” are an ISO 9001:2008 & 22000:2005 certified firm engaged in Manufacturing and Supplying a comprehensive assortment of Essential Oil, Fragrance Oil, Food Flavor, Seasoning, Natural Food Color, etc.
